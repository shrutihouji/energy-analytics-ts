Overview

The objective of this application is to provide an insight on energy analytics in order to optimize energy production, consumption, and management. This will lead to a sustainable energy system wherein the demand and supply of resources are in sync.The analysis of the resources help in finding trends, seasonality or any pattern. The forecasting can help in future storage and refinery capacity planning. This can help deciding organizatons to make informed decisions about the usage of a resource, to improve efficiency, reduce costs, and reduce detrimental effects on environment. We will be covering natural gas, electricity and pretroleum.
